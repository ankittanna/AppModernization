UtilitiesService
service in module appModernizationApp

Description

AVAILABLE SERVICES

The UtilitiesService consist of a services for Common operations on the Date and other common functionalities used across app. This service works accross the application and it just needs a dependency of UtilitiesService and a call to UTILITY_NAME method of the services with required parameters.

These are the list of services available within UtilitiesService.
